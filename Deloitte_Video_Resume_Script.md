## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Slide Design Guidelines:
- **Clean, professional aesthetic** - not flashy, emphasis on clarity
- **Consistent color palette** - blues, grays, accent color for key metrics
- **Generous white space** - avoid cluttered slides
- **High-contrast text** - ensure readability
- **Minimal animation** - let content speak, not transitions
- **Professional sans-serif font** - Helvetica, Inter, or similar

### Loom Recording Tips:
- **Lighting**: Face well-lit, avoid shadows
- **Background**: Clean, professional (consider virtual background if needed)
- **Audio**: Use quality microphone, minimize background noise
- **Eye contact**: Look at camera, not yourself in preview
- **Energy**: Conversational but professional tone
- **Pacing**: Speak clearly at moderate pace (not rushed)
- **Practice**: Run through 2-3 times before final recording

### Chronicle Slide Timing:
- **Title slides**: 5-10 seconds
- **Content slides with narration**: 20-40 seconds each
- **Complex diagrams**: 40-60 seconds
- **Transition slides**: 3-5 seconds
- **Total target**: 5-7 minutes (adjust pacing as needed)

### Key Moments to Emphasize:
1. **10x time compression** - this is your headline metric
2. **90-minute workshops** - tangible, memorable, impressive
3. **Governance frameworks** - shows you're not just fast, but responsible
4. **Business impact** - always connect back to outcomes, not just methods
5. **Cross-functional collaboration** - emphasize stakeholder engagement
6. **Scalable practices** - you build infrastructure, not one-off solutions
