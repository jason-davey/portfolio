# Content Strategy Context

## Case Study Structure Template
Each case study should follow this narrative framework:

### 1. Context & Challenge
- Business/design problem
- Stakeholders and constraints
- Success metrics defined

### 2. Approach & Process
- Design methodology used
- Collaboration approach
- Key decisions and rationale

### 3. Solution & Execution
- Final design/strategy
- Implementation details
- Team coordination

### 4. Impact & Learning
- Measurable outcomes
- Lessons learned
- Future applications

## Content Curation Goals
- Transform unfinished case studies into compelling narratives
- Maintain consistent voice and structure
- Optimize for both human readers and AI assistant integration
- Showcase design leadership evolution across different scales

## Voiceflow Integration Points
- Project deep-dives and process explanations
- Interactive Q&A about design approach
- Dynamic content based on visitor interests
- Personalized journey through adventure paths
